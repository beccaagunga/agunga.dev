Hey — my name is Becca and I'm a front-end web developer & digital designer from Columbus, Ohio. I'm passionate about building clean, minimal and responsive interfaces for the web. I enjoy exploring the capabilities of CSS to provide unique user experiences through design.

I'm a 2016 graduate of The Ohio State University where I studied Fashion & Retail Studies and Visual Communications. In early 2019, I completed a coding bootcamp where I quickly found passion for code & design.

When I'm not coding, you can find me enjoying time with friends and family, watching Browns highlights, or doing something active. I've been working for Vans for 3 years, I'm a big fan of skateboard culture and its' impact on fashion.
